Body Locations

Now, whenever you conduct combat, do so normally. And every normal blow struck is deducted from the character's hit points normally.

Every normal blow struck (that is, every blow not declared as aimed at a specific Body Location) hits the character's torso (i.e., any point from his shoulders to his hips).

However, if you take a Called Shot, you can make your strike at any one of six other Body Locations (head, two arms, two legs, and stun-points).
