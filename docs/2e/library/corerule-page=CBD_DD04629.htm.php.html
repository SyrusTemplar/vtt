Weapon Proficiencies and Specialization

Only single-class fighters may have weapon specialization. Multi-class fighter/priests and fighter/thieves have other benefits to offset their lack of specialization. The Complete Fighter's Handbook contains many new and useful forms of weapon specialization that will enhance the abilities of dwarf warriors.
