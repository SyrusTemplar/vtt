Modifying and Creating Kits

You and your DM may change the kits to customize them for your own campaign world, or even restrict the availability of some kits to NPCs. Similarly, feel free to create totally new kits. For guidelines on doing so, refer to the kit creation rules in The Complete Fighter's Handbook (PH, pgs. 37-38). However, make sure that no kit is more powerful than any in this book.
