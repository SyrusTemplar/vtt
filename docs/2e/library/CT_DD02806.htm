Move Earth

This spell can collapse ramparts (or sections of ramparts) up to 240 yards long and up to 60 feet thick. A rampart collapsed in this manner is treated as damaged; if less than a third of its original volume remains, it is considered destroyed (see the Sieges section, below). A rampart can be made resistant to move earth spells by facing it with brick or stone or bracing it internally with timbers or stone. A rampart reinforced in this manner resists a move earth spell on a roll of 10 or higher on 1d20. If the rampart is faced and internally braced, it is immune to move earth spells.
