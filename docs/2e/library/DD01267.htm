Magic Resistance

Most spells fail when used against a legendary monster with this power. The creature-s resistance can be continuous or conditional. For example, a creature might be resistant to spells only while the moon is full or for a short time after it eats a certain food. Similarly, a creature-s resistance might be ineffective against a certain class of spells or ineffective against opponents who have found a specific item or performed a special task.
