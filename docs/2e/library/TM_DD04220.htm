Dilation II (Alteration)

Range: 0

Component: V

Duration: Special

Casting Time: 5

Area of Effect: Special

Saving Throw: None

This spell functions exactly like the 4th-level dilation I spell, except that the area of effect of a 1st-, 2nd-, or 3rd- level spell is extended by 50%. Alternatively, the wizard may extend the area of effect of one 4th- or 5th-level spell by 25%.
