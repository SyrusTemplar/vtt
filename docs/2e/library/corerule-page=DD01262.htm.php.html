Invulnerability (Physical)

Not every weapon can harm a legendary monster. This power can work in several different ways:

Weapon Type: The creature suffers no damage from a single type of weapon (bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing).

Normal Weapon: The creature suffers no damage from ordinary weapons and is harmed only by weapons made from a special material (silver, cold-wrought iron, stone, wood, etc.).

Nonmagical Weapon: The creature can be harmed only by enchanted weapons. Lesser scions with this power usually can be harmed by +1 or better weapons. Scions and elders are harmed by +2 or better weapons, and great elders and paragons by +3 or better weapons.
