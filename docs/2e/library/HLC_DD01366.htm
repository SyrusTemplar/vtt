Rangers Beyond 20th Level

Extra Followers: At 21st level, a ranger attracts 2d6 more followers. The ranger attracts another group of 2d6 followers at 26th level.

Scroll and Magical Item Use: At 21st level, a ranger can read priest scrolls. The ranger's level is considered to be 9th when determining the chance of spell failure. The ranger can also use priest magical items at this level.

Scroll Writing: At 24th level, a ranger can prepare scrolls of priest spells according to the rules in Chapter 4. The ranger's level for purposes of determining success or failure is considered to be 9th.

Scrying: At 27th level, a ranger can employ crystal balls and other scrying devices as a 9th-level wizard.
