This System and Low-Level Characters

Low-level characters, with their small store of hit points, are comparatively easy to hurt in this system. A character with 10 hit points has a Numbing number of 3 and an Injuring number of 5. However, since these special damage results are all temporary, and add a certain amount of flavor to combats, you should have no difficulty using them with characters of any level.

However, don't forget that all these special shots must be Called Shots. They don't come about because of random die-rolls.
