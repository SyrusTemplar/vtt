Saw of Mighty Cutting: This notched adamantite blade is 12 feet long and over 1 foot wide. It requires 18/00 or greater Strength to operate alone, or two people of 17 or greater Strength working in tandem. The blade will slice through a 1-foot diameter tree in three rounds, a 2-foot thick hardwood tree in one turn, or a 4-foot thick trunk in three turns. After six turns (cumulative) of cutting with the saw, the character or characters must rest for six turns before doing any further work.
