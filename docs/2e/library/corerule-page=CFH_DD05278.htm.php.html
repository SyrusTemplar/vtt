The Locations

These are the character's Body Locations:

Torso: As described above. The Torso is hit with any non-Called Shot.

Head: Must be hit with a Called Shot at an additional –4 to attack rolls.

Arms (2): One right, one left. Must be hit with a Called Shot.

Legs (2): One right, one left. Must be hit with a Called Shot.

Stun-Points: This rather broad category includes the solar plexus and other nerve centers which, when struck, tend to cause the victim a lot of pain. Stun-Points must be hit with a Called Shot at an additional –4 to attack rolls.
