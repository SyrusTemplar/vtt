Phobia?Heights: This character has difficulty climbing ladders and ropes, perching on walls, and negotiating steep, cliff-side trails. He will be eager to look for another way around if such a climb is called for, but if he makes a successful Wisdom/Willpower check he can overcome his fear. If the check fails, however, the character will do everything possible to avoid the climb. Another check is allowed 2?12 turns later.
