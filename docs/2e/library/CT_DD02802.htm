Lightning Bolt

This spell damages wooden constructions just as a fireball does. It inflicts 1d2 points of structural damage against stone or metal targets for every three dice of damage it would normally inflict; round fractions down. For example, a lightning bolt from a 7th-level caster would inflict 3d2 points of damage against a mantlet (a wooden target) and 2d2 points of damage against a portcullis (a metal target). A lightning bolt can set wooden constructions afire just as a fireball can.
