Combat Rules

The Warrior is a character whose primary function is combat, and combat is what this chapter is all about. Though the AD&D? game combat system is sufficient to most of your combat needs, this chapter will give you optional rules and advice which can add flavor to your game.

It's important to remember that everything in this chapter is an optional rule; nothing recommended here can be implemented in your campaign without the DM's permission.
