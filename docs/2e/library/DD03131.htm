Phobia-Enclosed Spaces: This has effects similar to the darkness phobia, though of course the presence of light is immaterial-this phobia can strike in a lighted room or in a narrow, winding tunnel. In general, when the ceiling is no more than two feet overhead, and the walls are within two feet of the character's outstretched hands, he-ll have trouble. As with the fear of darkness, the character must check Wisdom/Willpower before entering an enclosed area.
