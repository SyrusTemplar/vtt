Wand of Enemy Detection: This wand pulses in the wielder's hand and points in the direction of any creature(s) hostile to the bearer of the device. The creature(s) can be invisible, ethereal, astral, out of phase, hidden, disguised, or in plain sight. Detection range is a 60-foot sphere. The function requires one charge to operate for oneturn. The wand can be recharged.
