iquid Road: When sprinkled on water, swampland, quicksand, or a similar surface, liquid road causes the terrain to harden to the density of granite, enabling easy passage. Liquid road is also effective in negating the effects of spells such as transmute rock to mud. The liquid road stays hard for one hour, after which the terrain returns to its original state. One flask of liquid road can harden a 5'x 5' surface (for example, a path 25' long and 1' wide).
