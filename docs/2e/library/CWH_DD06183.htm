The Restricted Level Campaign

Similar to the Restricted School Campaign, in this variation, high-level magic does not exist. For instance, the DM might decide that no spells of 6th level or higher exist. Therefore, a wizard has access to spells of 1st level through 5th level only, and he is unable to research higher-level spells. As with the Restricted School Campaign, the DM decides whether magical devices duplicating the effects of higher-level spells exist.
