Ideas for New Personality Types

The personality types are not confined to the ones suggested above. Here are a few other possibilities:

? The Sidekick (a trusted companion and assistant to a player character).

? The Compromiser (a superb negotiator, dedicated to preserving harmony and good relations among his companions).

? The Coward (a reluctant fighter, frightened of everything and always expecting the worst).

? The Carefree Wanderer (a happy-go-lucky wayfarer).

? The Sneak (a wizard who loves to trick people and take advantage of their naivete).

? The Chronicler (a historian devoted to making a permanent, detailed record of his party's activities)
