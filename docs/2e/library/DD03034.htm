Carpentry: This character knows the basics of working with wood and can create-with no check required-small structures, fences, platforms, cabinets, carts and wagons. The carpenter can make wooden wheels, but a blacksmith must form the iron rim or the wheel will have a very short life expectancy.

A carpenter might build a short footbridge, a wooden clock, or a dumbwaiter system-these tasks will require a proficiency check. Larger projects such as major bridges, boats, or catapults, require the aid of a character with the engineering proficiency.
