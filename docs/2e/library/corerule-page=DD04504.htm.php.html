Prism of Light Splitting: This useful device refracts light into the three primary colors of light--red, blue, and green. The user can choose the color of light that is emitted by the prism.

When creating a magical fluid or powder, the wizard casts the enchant an item spell. Following this, the wizard may use the prism of light splitting to shine a blue, red, or green beam of light on the mixture. The light must shine on the substance for one full day. At the end of this time, the material gains an additional magical property, depending upon the color of the beam employed.

Red:
The potion, powder, or aromatic oil is stronger than normal; targets of its

effects suffer a -2 saving throw penalty.
Blue:
The duration of the magical potion, powder, or fragrance's effect is

doubled.
Green:
The amount of liquid or powder is doubled; the wizard now has enough

for two potions, powders, or aromatic oils.
