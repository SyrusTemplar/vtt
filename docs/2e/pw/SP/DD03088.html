Weaving: A character with this skill can weave yarn into cloth, and he can create tapestries, cloaks, and other large swaths from thread. The character can spin wool into yarn with a spinning wheel, and he needs a loom to artfully weave that yarn. A character with the artistic talent trait can use this skill to create exceptionally beautiful cloth. Halflings get a +1 bonus to their rating with this proficiency.
