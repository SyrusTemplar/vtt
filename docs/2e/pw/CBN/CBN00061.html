Unnatural Ability Scores

The necromancer has engaged in research to augment his or her physical and mental attributes, raising them above their normal scores (by means of discovered wishes, magical tomes, enchanted pools, and so on). This research results in a total of 1–3 additional point, which may be distributed by the DM among the necromancer’s ability scores. Alternatively, the DM may distribute each additional ability point by randomly rolling a d12: 1) Strength; 2–5) Intelligence; 6–7) Wisdom; 8–9) Dexterity; 10–11) Constitution; 12) Charisma.
