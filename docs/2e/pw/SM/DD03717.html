Appendix 3: Wizard Spells by School

Italicized spells are reversible. Boldfaced spells can be found in the Tome of Magic . SMALL CAPITALIZED spells appear in The Complete Wizard’s Handbook. Underlined spells are introduced in Appendix 1 of this book. As mentioned in Chapter 2 , some spells have been reassigned to the school of universal magic.
