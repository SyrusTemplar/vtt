Globe of Purification: These enchanted glass spheres, 6 inches in diameter, contain the swirling blue-grey essence of a cloud of purification spell. When the globe is broken, the cloud billows forth, acting exactly as if the spell had been cast by a 12th-level priest. These devices are often given to a city's sanitation crews, who descend upon the streets and sewers during the wee hours of the morning.
