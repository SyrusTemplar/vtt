Far Reaching III (Alteration)

Range: 0

Component: V

Duration: Special

Casting Time: 5

Area of Effect: Special

Saving Throw: None

This spell operates exactly like the 3rd-level far reaching I spell except that the range of any spell of levels 1-3 is increased by 150% and the range of any 4th- or 5th-level spell is increased by 50%.
