Ring of Fortitude: When worn, the ring of fortitude grants the wearer a bonus of +4 to one randomly selected ability score (roll 1d12: 1-6=Dexterity, 7-11=Wisdom, 12=Constitution) for the purposes of spell resolution only. It does not affect ability checks or other aspects of ability scores, except those as a direct result of spells or spell-like abilities.

For example, a character with a natural Constitution of 14 would have an enhanced score of 18 while wearing such a ring. He does not gain any extra hit points from wearing it, but his system shock for resolving the polymorph other spell is increased to 99%. With the Constitution-enhancing ring, the bonuses even apply to the raise dead and resurrection spells. All effects are lost when the wearer removes the ring.
