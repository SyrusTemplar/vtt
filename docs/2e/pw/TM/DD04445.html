Ring of Armoring: A wizard wearing this ring gains an additional +1 bonus to any AC bonus he receives from casting a spell upon himself. Thus, an armor spell grants the wizard AC 5 instead of AC 6, and a shield spell grants the wizard AC 1 versus hand-hurled missiles instead of AC 2. Restrictions that apply to a spell (for example, armor does not affect a character already wearing armor) are in no way altered through use of this ring.
