Wand of Fear: When the fear wand is activated, a pale amber ray springs from the tip of the wand, forming a cone 60 feet long by 20 feet in base diameter, which flashes on and instantly disappears. Each creature touched by the ray must roll a successful saving throw vs. wand or react as per the cause fear spell (1st-level priest spell, remove fear rversal). In other words, creatures affected by the wand turn and move at fastest possible speed away from the wielder for six rounds. Each use costs one charge. It can operate just once per round. The wand can be recharged.
